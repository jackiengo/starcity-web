Oakland

Situated in Downtown Oakland, this community is a historic hotel conversion near the 12th St. Bart station. The building will have communal kitchens on each floor and large social spaces to relax. It will also feature a bar, restaurant and coffee shop on the ground floor. It will be ready for occupancy in late Winter / early Spring 2018.
